    About Us

    Why we started Vulterm?

    We are a group of entrepreneurs and legal technologist who have discovered that SMEs are having a tough time getting their policies and terms of use right. There has been issues of incomplete T&C, unreadable and incomprehensive terms of use and so many issues.

    We therefore came together, researched on didgital laws,compile them and finally categorized them based on location and type of business.

    We have decided to take it further and created the "Per annum Package". This package includes everything from basic you need to know concerning legal issues for your business. We also give you the HTML code, PDF, TXT, DOCX file of your privacy policy and Terms of Service.
