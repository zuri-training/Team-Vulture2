Why you need Terms of Service

Having a good Terms of Service(TOS) is very important for every website or application.
Basically, it is a legal agreement between a service and a person that wants to use that
service.
We will take care to clear all the doubts of your current and upcoming users with our highly
professional TOS generator.

A terms and condition agreement protects your intellectual property rights, content
liability, and gives guidelines on how website information like cookies will be used.
